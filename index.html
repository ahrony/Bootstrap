Hello, world! I love Bootstrap, but personally if I had to build a fixed-width 960px site, which is quite old school nowadays, I wouldn’t use Bootstrap at all. And you know what? In most cases I wouldn’t even use a grid system! I’d use plain-old CSS (or Sass), and I’m pretty confident it would be fine. But that’s me. Of course you are free to do anything you want. But remember, Bootstrap’s focus is on mobile and responsive design.
